# Executive Insights

Key findings:
- Fraud is concentrated in high-risk merchant categories such as electronics, travel, and entertainment.
- High-value transactions and odd-hour purchases show a higher proportion of fraud.
- Customer risk segments reveal that a small fraction of customers account for the majority of flagged transactions.

Recommendations:
- Deploy targeted monitoring for high-risk merchants and high-value transactions.
- Use automated alerts on odd-hour or unusual transaction patterns.
- Combine fraud scorecards with customer risk segments for stronger detection.
