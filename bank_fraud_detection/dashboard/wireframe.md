# Bank Fraud Detection Dashboard Wireframe

The dashboard will show:
- Fraud rate by transaction type and merchant category
- High risk customer segments
- Daily transaction volume and fraud spike detection
- Key statistical summary and next-step recommendations

Wireframe sections:
1. Hero: project name, description, and quick stats
2. Fraud overview: risk by merchant category and transaction type
3. Customer risk segments
4. Data pipeline and methodology
5. Executive summary and business insights
