# Social Media Content Strategy by Day

Use this guide to decide which content category should lead each day of the week.

| Day | Recommended content type | Suggested purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Food | Start the week with a warm, relatable post that feels fresh and engaging. |
| Tuesday | Facts | Share quick educational insights or interesting knowledge bites. |
| Wednesday | Movies | Highlight trailers, reviews, or pop-culture moments. |
| Thursday | Cartoons | Post light, entertaining, and visually appealing content. |
| Friday | News | Share timely updates or trending headlines to capture attention. |
| Saturday | Food + Movies | Blend entertainment with lifestyle content for a fun weekend vibe. |
| Sunday | Recap | Summarize the week, share favorites, and encourage audience interaction. |

## Practical posting rule
- Keep the content mix balanced across education, entertainment, and lifestyle.
- Pair strong visuals with short captions for better engagement.
- Use the weekday theme as a planning guide, then adapt it to your audience.
