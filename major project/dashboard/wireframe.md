# Dashboard Wireframe

## Page 1: Summary
- KPI cards: Total Engagement, Positive Sentiment Ratio, Top Hashtag, Peak Day
- Charts: Engagement by Platform, Sentiment Distribution, Weekly Trend
- Layout: 3-column grid with executive KPIs at the top and two charts below

## Page 2: Sentiment
- Charts: Sentiment by Platform, Sentiment over Time, Topic-level sentiment breakdown
- Filters: Platform, Date Range, Hashtag
- Layout: Left column for filters, center for trend chart, right for breakdown

## Page 3: Trend Velocity
- Charts: Weekly engagement velocity, rolling 7-day averages, anomaly spikes
- Drill-through: Post-level details for selected spikes
- Layout: Full-width trend chart with supporting KPI cards underneath
